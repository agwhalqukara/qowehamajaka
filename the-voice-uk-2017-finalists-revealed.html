The Voice UK 2017 finalists revealed

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Since the beginning of the year, we have watched the build up to the grand final where one hopeful will be crowned The Voice UK 2017 winner and will win the all important Polydor record deal.

After several weeks of blind auditions, the dreaded battle rounds, live knockouts and live quarter and semi-finals, it’s all built up to the live final that will air next Saturday where the four finalists will compete against each other.

First revealed as a finalist was 19 year old Welsh singer Jamie Miller from Jennifer Hudson’s team. Next we saw former backing singer Michelle John go through to the final. Michelle has been a constant hit with the public since appearing on the show.

Both of Jennifer Hudson’s acts are finalists as last night also saw Mo Adeniran go through to next week’s final after his performance of Rag’N’Bone Man’s song ‘Human’. The last space in the final was taken up by Welsh duo ‘Into The Ark’ from Tom Jones’ team.

Missing out on a spot in the final, Truly Ford, Max Vickers, Jason Jones and Craig Ward were all sent home on last night’s show. CelebMix would like to wish them all the luck in the future.

The Bush singer and coach Gavin Rossdale is the only coach this year not to have any of his acts in the final. Jennifer Hudson has two acts in the final with Will and Tom both having one.

All four finalists will battle out in next week’s final on ITV for that all important winners spot and highly respected Polydor record deal.

The Voice UK 2017 final is live Saturday 1st April at 8.30pm on ITV.
