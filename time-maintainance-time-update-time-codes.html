Time, Maintainance Time, Update Time, Codes

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Honkai Star Rail 1.4 Release Date: Honkail Star Rail, the popular sci-fi action RPG from HoYoverse, is getting a major update on October 11, 2023. The update, dubbed Version 1.4, will introduce new characters, new areas, new events, and more to the game. Here is everything you need to know about Honkai Star Rail 1.4, including the release time, maintenance time, update time, codes, countdown, and live stream.

Honkai Star Rail 1.4 Release Time and Maintenance Time

According to the official announcement from HoYoverse, Honkai Star Rail, 1.4 will go live on October 11, 2023, at 6 a.m. (UTC+8). However, this means that the update will be released on October 10 in some regions, such as Europe and North America.

The game will undergo a necessary maintenance break to get ready for the new content that is coming soon. The maintenance will start on October 10 at 10 p.m. (UTC-5) and will last for approximately five hours. During this time, players will not be able to access the game.

Update Time and Preload

After the maintenance is over, players will be able to download and install the update to enjoy the new features of Honkai Star Rail Version 1.4. The update size is estimated to be around 2 GB, so make sure you have enough storage space on your device.

To save time and avoid network congestion, players can also choose to preload the update before it goes live. The preload option is available from October 9 and can be accessed from the game’s main menu. By preloading the update, players can reduce the download time and start playing as soon as the update is released.

New Characters

Two new 5-Star Characters, Topaz (and Numby) and Jingliu will join the Event Warps in Version 1.4.

Both of them are powerful and unique characters that can enhance your team’s performance in different situations.

Topaz (and Numby)

Topaz is a Cybernetic Engineer who uses a Mechanical Arm as her weapon. She is accompanied by her loyal companion Numby, a Cybernetic Cat that can transform into various forms and assist her in combat. Topaz and Numby have a Symbiotic Relationship, which means that they share their Energy and Health. Topaz can use her Mechanical Arm to perform various attacks, such as Punching, Grabbing, Throwing, and Shooting. She can also use Numby’s forms to unleash powerful skills, such as Numby Bomb, Numby Shield, and Numby Drill. Topaz and Numby are a versatile duo that can adapt to different scenarios and deal massive damage to their enemies.

Topaz (and Numby) will be available in the Topaz and Numby Event Warp from October 11 to October 25. Players can use Event Warp Tickets or Stellar Jades to obtain them.

Jingliu

Jingliu is a Martial Artist who uses a Sword as her weapon. She is a master of the Jade Dragon Style, a martial art that focuses on speed, agility, and precision. Jingliu can use her sword to perform swift and graceful attacks, such as Slash, Stab, Parry, and Counter. She can also use her Jade Dragon Energy to activate her special skills, such as Jade Dragon Slash, Jade Dragon Strike, and Jade Dragon Burst. Jingliu is a fast and elegant character who can outwit and overpower her opponents with her skillful swordplay.

Jingliu will be available in the Jingliu Event Warp from October 11 to October 25. Players can use Event Warp Tickets or Stellar Jades to obtain her.

New Areas

There are two new areas introduced in Version 1.4 for Honkai: Star Rail:

  • Jarilo-VI
  • Swarm Disaster

Jarilo-VI

Jarilo-VI is a planet that hosts the Aetherium Wars Festival, an offline event for fans of the most influential online gaming brand in the universe, Aetherium Wars. The festival features various activities, such as cosplay contests, merchandise booths, live performances, and more. However, the main attraction of the festival is the Interastral Tournament, a competition where players can participate in real-life battles using their favorite Aetherium Wars characters and weapons. The tournament is divided into four categories: Solo, Duo, Squad, and Guild. The winners of each category will receive exclusive prizes and fame.

Jarilo-VI is a colorful and lively area that offers a lot of fun and excitement for players. Players can explore the festival grounds, interact with other fans, join the tournament, and enjoy the Aetherium Wars experience.

Swarm Disaster

Swarm Disaster is a new mode that challenges players to survive against waves of enemies in a post-apocalyptic setting. The model is divided into three stages: Escape, Survive, and Fight Back. In each stage, players will face different types of enemies, such as zombies, mutants, robots, and more. Players will also have to deal with environmental hazards, such as traps, explosions, and radiation. The mode will test players’ skills, strategies, and teamwork.

Swarm Disaster is a thrilling and intense mode that offers a lot of action and rewards for players. Players can earn Swarm Coins by completing stages and challenges in the mode. Swarm Coins can be used to exchange for various items in the Swarm Shop, such as weapons, outfits, materials, and more.

During the event, players can receive various gifts and rewards by logging in daily, completing missions, and participating in activities. Some of the rewards include:

Gift of Odyssey is an event that shows appreciation and gratitude to the players for their support and love for Honkai: Star Rail.

Codes, Countdown, and Live Stream

To celebrate the release of Version 1.4, HoYoverse has also prepared some codes, a countdown, and a live stream for the players.

HoYoverse has released several codes that can be redeemed for free rewards in Honkai: Star Rail. The codes are valid until October 31 and can be redeemed by entering them in the Settings menu under the Redeem Code option. The codes are:

  • HSR14-GIFT: Redeem for 500 Stellar Jades, 5 Event Warp Tickets, and 1 Anniversary Cake
  • HSR14-AETH: Redeem for 1000 Aetherium Coins, 10 Aetherium Crystals, and 1 Aetherium Outfit
  • HSR14-FUTU: Redeem for 1000 Future Coins, 10 Future Materials, and 1 Future Weapon

The live stream will feature some gameplay footage, developer commentary, a Q&A session, and more. Players who watch the livestream will also have a chance to win some exclusive prizes, such as:
