What is Gary Brolsma's Net Worth?

Gary Brolsma is an American singer and actor who has a net worth of $50 thousand dollars. He gained worldwide popularity after posting a video of himself doing a lipdub dance of the song "Dragostea din tei" from the Moldovan pop trio O-Zone Gary Brolsma. Born on January 14, 1986 in Saddle Brook, New Jersey, he has come to prominence as an internet phenomenon. Literally speaking, he became a guru in making amateur videos. Brolsma's first video, in which he makes a lip-singing of the song "Dragostea din tei" was uploaded on the website Newgrounds.com in December 2004 under the title "Numa Numa Song". Several months later, it reached viewership of more than two million people. After Brolmsa gained widespread popularity for his video he was interviewed and made appearances in major shows in the United States, including "Good Morning America" and "The Tonight Show".

His video was ranked as Number 1 on the Top 40 Greatest Internet Stars by VH1, beating the Star Wars Kid video which was ranked as Number 2. By October 2008, Brolsma released his first album titled "Weird Tempo" which is a combination of electronic beat with rock influences. Brolsma has participated in short comedy films and comedy shows. His short filmography includes a TV Movie comedy called "Rude Tube" and a short comedy: "Between the Lines: The Rise and Fall of Tobias". Brolsma had a minor role in the 2015 fantasy film "The Chronicles of Rick Roll". He has released online albums titled Weird Tempo, Project Flamingo, and Haunted House of Pancakes which are available on iTunes and SoundCloud.
