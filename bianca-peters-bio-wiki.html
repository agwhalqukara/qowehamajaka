Bianca Peters Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Who is Bianca Peters?

Bianca Peters is an American News Anchor and Journalist in Miami, Florida.

Currently, Bianca works as the news anchor for the TV station, FOX 5 in NYC. Peters is the co-host of Good Day Wakeup weekdays from 4:30 a.m. – 7 a.m.

Previously, she worked as a sports news and traffic anchor for  CBS4 Miami.

She is also the founder of Castillo Social Co.

Who are the parents of Bianca Peters?

Bianca was born on April 1st, 1991 in Miami, Florida USA. As of 2023, she is 29 years old.

Her birth name is Bianca Castillo Peters. She is the second daughter of Anthony M. Peters and Patrica Peters.

She has two siblings, a sister Moria, and a brother Anthony Ezra.

Bianca Peters gained her degree from California State University, Long Beach, CA in Journalism. While in college, she worked for College Beat, producing and hosting a college football show.

In 2010, Bianca Peters has crowned Miss Malibu. She also participated in Miss California 2010 and secured her position in the Top 10 finalists.

Bianca Peters: Professional Career

Bianca started her career in broadcasting as a sports anchor and reporter for College Beat Productions in Long Beach, California.

In September 2019, she was assigned as a news anchor in FOX 5.

Before joining FOX, she worked at WFOR-TV (CBS4 Miami)  in Miami, Florida, where she served as a traffic anchor and sports reporter for 3 years (2016- 2019).

Before that, Bianca Peters was a news anchor and reporter for KOBI-TV in Medford, Oregon.

Previously, she held the position of host and segment producer for JUCE TV in Tustin, California.

Prior to that, Peters worked at ESPN where she served as a host and reporter for ESPN’s X Games in Los Angeles, California.

She has also worked as a morning show anchor and reporter for NBC5, KOTI, and FOX 26 all based in the south of Oregon.
